Financial Analysis Report – FESTMAN STORES

This project focuses on analysing FESTMAN STORES financial data using Power BI to uncover insights into sales performance, profitability, customer segments, discounts, and product performance.

The dashboard was built to help understand business trends and identify areas affecting revenue and profit growth.

Tool Used
Power BI

Key Insights
Sales increased significantly compared to the previous year
Germany recorded the highest profit margin
High discount bands contributed the largest percentage of sales
Paseo was the top-performing product by sales
Small Business recorded the lowest profit margin among customer segments

Dashboard Features
KPI cards for Sales, Profit, Orders and Profit Margin
Profit Margin by Country
Sales Trend Analysis by Month and Year
Top Products by Sales
Discount Band Analysis
Segment Performance Analysis
